Olympics

The opening ceremony to signal the official start of the Paris 2024 Olympics will be held on Friday, 26 July. But the sporting action actually begins on Wednesday, 24 July with pool matches in football, rugby sevens, handball and archery. The ambitious opening ceremony will take place along the River Seine instead of in a stadium. The first medals will be awarded a day later and competition will conclude on 11 August, with the closing ceremony later that evening.
